Our Story

Meaningful
Moments in Public Spaces

We believe the most important stories deserve the most visible stages. Dropcast is the platform that makes public screen advertising accessible — not just for big brands, but for artists, creators, and changemakers.

"What podcasts did for radio, and what Canva did for design, we're doing for public screens: making them accessible to the stories that matter."

01

The Problem

Public screens — billboards, transit displays, digital kiosks — have always been the domain of corporations with massive budgets. The stories that actually matter rarely make it to the streets.

02

Our Answer

Dropcast is a platform that democratises access to public screens. We give artists, independent brands, and social enterprises the tools and reach previously reserved for multinationals.

03

Who We're For

Artists launching exhibitions. Brands building community. NGOs amplifying causes. Musicians dropping albums. Anyone with something real to say and the courage to say it loudly.

04

The Vision

A world where the screens in our shared spaces reflect the full richness of our culture — not just those who can afford a traditional agency. Public space, for the public good.

The Process

From idea to
public screen

  • 01

    Tell us your story

    Fill out our brief or drop us an email. Tell us who you are, what you're trying to say, and who you want to say it to. No jargon required.

  • 02

    We craft the campaign

    Our team helps shape your content for public screens — the right format, the right messaging, the right visual approach to stop people in their tracks.

  • 03

    Choose your screens

    Select from our network of digital billboards, transit displays, and digital kiosks. Choose locations by city, footfall, audience, or vibe.

  • 04

    Go live

    Your story goes up. We send you photos, impressions data, and the quiet satisfaction of knowing your work is being seen in the real world.

Your content, deployed across billboards, kiosks, and transit screens in your chosen cities.
